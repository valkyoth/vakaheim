## [0.1.0] - Unreleased

### Added

- Security-first Rust workspace with three dependency-free `no_std` crates.
- Architecture constitution and detailed road to production 1.0.
- EUPL-1.2 licensing and strict no-publication policy.
- Repository, GitHub, security, modularity, toolchain, and release gates.
- Linux, Windows, macOS, BSD, Android, iOS, and future Aesynx portability plan.

### Security

- Unsafe Rust forbidden in current crates.
- Third-party runtime, build, and development crates prohibited.
- Exact-commit pentest required before any tag.
